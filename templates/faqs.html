General questions

What is guessing and how does it work?

Guessing (and the allow guesses option) enables the person who is tagged in the post to try and guess the uni of the person who tagged them. If they guess correctly, an email will be sent letting both the poster and the person tagged know that they matched. What happens next is up to you ;)

Is it REALLY anonymous?

Yes absolutely! (Unless you uncheck the 'anonymous' checkbox of course). If the anonymous checkbox is filled we NEVER store the poster's information regardless of if they are logged in or not.

Can I have more than three guesses?

As of now, guesses are limited to 3, but we might allow the user to choose the number of guesses in the future.
